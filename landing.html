Focus on what matters.

Distill your programs to their essence and solve the problems you care about. Let the language take care of the details. Arevel automatically manages your application memory, distributes concurrent tasks across cores, infers type signatures and even solves whole classes of logic and constraint problems for you.

Designed to evolve with you.

From prototype to production, Arevel adapts to the ever-changing requirements of a growing project. Add contractual guarantees to your program with gradual types, isolate complex functionality with independent modules or even transform the language itself with macros.

Let's build it together.

Software is a collaborative medium. Arevel gives you the tools to create great software together across the internet.
